Cursed Jumper

A game I developed for a jam in 2024 using the Godot engine. The game itself is a 2D platformer with randomly generated gameplay constraints. I was one of two programmers, with a focus on core gameplay systems and UI functionality.

TANK Go Crazy!

A game I developed solo with the Godot engine. I was responsible for all graphics, sounds, animations, and programming.

Spellwarp

A narrative clicker game I helped create in the Godot engine in the span of a week during 2023. I served as the lead developer, and coordinated the development process from brainstorming to final polish.

Cipher Capsule

An educational simulator I developed as my senior capstone project with a partner in the Unity engine. The simulator is an accurate recreation of a Word War II era encryption machine. My role was creating 3D models, integrating the encryption script with the engine, and verifying the output's accuracy.
